Beginning of My Data Science Journey

  • By Tanu Namdeo

Hello everyone, Tanu this side. I’m quite nervous about writing my first blog because I don’t know how it’s going to turn out — but let’s take the first step. I read somewhere that your first podcast will be bad, your first video will be bad, and whatever you do for the first time will probably be bad. But your 100th attempt won’t be the same as your first. So, with this quote, let me share my data science journey.

So currently, I’m in my 2nd year, and it's the end of my 4th semester. In the initial days, I was very confused about what to do — I felt stuck in different things. At first, I decided to become a data analyst. But after exploring Excel, I thought, “This is not for me.” However, while learning it, I was actually understanding the basics of Excel, which now help me in tracking my progress. I’ll share more about that in another blog, but for now, let’s stay on topic.
